PC Build

November 7, 2018


I thought it would be interesting to do a build using the Mini-ITX form factor. I sourced parts from Newegg, Amazon, and B&H Photo. I wasn't able to use the SSD tray due to using a 140mm fan, but there is a place on the bottom of the case to mount a SSD. I was able to use the stock AM4 cooler because of the SFX power supply. I reused the GTX 970 and SSD from a previous build.

Parts List:

SilverStone Sugo SG13B Mini-Tower Case $043.99
ASRock Fatal1ty AB350 Gaming-ITX/ac AM4 $104.99
AMD RYZEN 7 1700 $179.99
XPG GAMMIX D10 16GB (2 x 8GB) 288-Pin DDR4 SDRAM DDR4 2666 $119.99
Silicon Power Ace A55 2.5" 1TB SATA III 3D NAND $149.99
Zotac ZT-90101-10P GeForce GTX 970 $243.83
Silverstone Tek 140mm Ultra-Quiet PWM Fan $019.99
SilverStone Technology 450W SFX Form Factor 80 PLUS GOLD Full Modular Power Supply $093.99
