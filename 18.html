Beyerdynamic DT 770 PRO Review

July 31, 2019


The Beyerdynamic DT 770 PRO are a closed studio dynamic headphone. The ear pads are very comfortable and they sound pretty good. I think they might sound better with a dedicated DAC/Amp. What I like about these headphones is they are very comfortable for extended listening. They are however bulky so probably not very good if you're looking for portable headphones.

Specs are as follows:

Frequency Response: 5 Hz - 35 kHz
Transducers: Dynamic
Noise Isolation: approx. 18 dBA
Impedance: 80 Ohms
Nominal SPL: 96 dB
Power handling capacity: 100 mW
Cable: 3 m / straight cable
Warranty: 2 Years
